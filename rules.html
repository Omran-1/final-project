Basketball Rules

Objective of the Game

Score more points than your opponent by the end of the game.

Scoring

  • 1 point: Free throws
  • 2 points: Field goals inside the 3-point line
  • 3 points: Shots beyond the arc

Game Format

  • NBA: 4 quarters × 12 minutes
  • NCAA: 2 halves × 20 minutes
  • FIBA: 4 quarters × 10 minutes

Includes timeouts, halftime, and overtime basics.

Basic Violations

  • Traveling
  • Double dribble
  • Carry / Palming
  • Backcourt violations

Fouls

  • Personal foul: Illegal contact
  • Technical foul: Unsportsmanlike behavior
  • Flagrant foul: Excessive or dangerous contact

Free Throws & Penalty System

  • Shooting fouls result in free throws
  • “Bonus” / “Penalty” situations after team fouls
  • Team fouls reset each quarter/half
