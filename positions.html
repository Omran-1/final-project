Basketball Positions

Point Guard (PG)

  • Primary ballhandler
  • Runs the offense
  • Responsible for playmaking

Shooting Guard (SG)

  • Scorer and perimeter shooter
  • Moves without the ball
  • Often a secondary ballhandler

Small Forward (SF)

  • Versatile, all-around player
  • Defends multiple positions
  • Can drive, shoot, and create plays

Power Forward (PF)

  • Strong inside presence
  • Rebounding and inside scoring
  • Mid-range shooting in modern play

Center (C)

  • Rim protector
  • Post scoring
  • Anchors the defense

Modern Basketball Roles

  • Stretch 4: Shooting power forward
  • Point Forward: Forward who initiates offense
  • Combo Guard: Guard who blends PG/SG skills
  • Small-ball Center: Undersized but versatile center
